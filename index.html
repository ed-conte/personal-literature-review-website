Welcome!

Greetings, book enthusiasts! Welcome to Fiction Fervor, a website dedicated to my passion for reading where literary exploration is not just encouraged but celebrated. Here, I pride myself on offering reviews, and synopsises of different historical literary works in the select few genres that I enjoy the most. I hope that you find this website to be a useful resource for finding your next favorite book, or for learning more about what others think about a book that you have already read. I hope you enjoy this website, and that it inspires you to read one of these books for yourself.

About Me

Hello, My name is Edward Conte. I am an undergraduate student at Cornell University. I have been an avid reader since I was a child, and I have been writing my own stories since middle school. I have had a passion for the art of storytelling since I was old enough to understand what a story was, and have the utmost appreciation for them in all of the various forms in which they has been expressed throughout the ages; however books reign supreme. stemming from my interest in history, I have a particular fondness for classic literature, epic poems, gothic horror and fantasy novels.
