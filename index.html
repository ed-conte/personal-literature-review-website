Welcome!

Greetings, book enthusiasts! Welcome to Fiction Fervor, a website dedicated to my passion for reading where literary exploration is not just encouraged but celebrated. Here, I pride myself on offering analysis, reviews, and synopsises of different literary works in the select few genres that I enjoy the most. I hope that you find this website to be a useful resource for finding your next favorite book, or for learning more about a book that you have already read. I hope you enjoy this website, and that it inspires you to read one of these books for yourself.

About Me

Hello, My name is Edward Conte. I am an undergraduate student at Cornell University. I have been an avid reader since I was very young, and have been writing my own stories since middle school. The advent of my passion for the art of storytelling began as a child, and persisted until my reading level was advanced enough to truly enjoy the finest literature. I have the utmost appreciation for storytelling in all of its various forms; however books reign supreme. Stemming from my interest in history, I have a particular fondness for the genres of classic literature, epic poetry, gothic horror and medieval fantasy.
