About the Dark Souls trilogy

Developed by FromSoftware in 2011, the infamous Dark Souls kicked off one of gaming's most influential trilogies. The Dark Souls games remain one of the most beloved series ever developed, boasting unique interactive storytelling, deep, gripping characters, and masterful worldbuilding that would inspire even the likes of George R. R. Martin to work with FromSoftware and create the award-winning Elden Ring. The Dark Souls trilogy, coupled with Fromsoftware's remaining portfolio of masterpieces: Demon's Souls, Bloodborne, Sekiro, and Elden Ring, are called the Soulsborne series and are considered by many the peak of the gaming medium. This series has not only won numerous Game of the Year awards but spawned a new gaming genre.
